Pavan’s 4-Step Method

Use this order for every problem.

1

Keywords

Circle words such as top K, longest substring, next greater, prerequisite, number of ways.

2

Constraints

Check sorted, negative values, cannot modify, O(1) space, weighted edges, and input size.

3

Pattern

Choose the pattern only after keywords and constraints agree.

4

Explain

State why the pattern works and give time and space complexity.
